# Ola-Ride-Booking-Analysis
# Objective:
Analyze booking patterns, revenue trends, cancellations, ride performance, and customer behavior to identify operational and business insights.

Pandas
NumPy
Matplotlib
Jupyter Notebook
Power BI 
# Workflow
-Data Loading & Inspection
-Data cleaning
-outlier analysis
-feature engineering
-EDA analysis
-Data export
# key insights
-Ride demand varies significantly throughout the day, with peak booking hours generating the highest activity.
-Revenue trends closely follow booking volume, highlighting critical business hours.
-Certain vehicle categories contribute a larger share of revenue while maintaining lower cancellation rates.
-A small number of pickup locations account for a significant portion of total bookings and revenue.
-Revenue loss is concentrated during periods with higher cancellation rates.
Success rates differ across time periods, indicating opportunities for operational improvement.
-Distance-based ride segmentation reveals varying customer preferences and revenue contribution patterns.
